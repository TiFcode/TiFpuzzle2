Privacy Policy for TiFpuzzle

Last updated: January 04, 2026

TiFpuzzle does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data or information from users.

This app is completely offline and does not connect to the internet or send any information to any servers, third parties, or external services.

No user data is transmitted, tracked, or processed in any way.

Permissions

Photo Library Access

The app needs access to your photo library to enable you to select photos for the puzzle game.

  • Photos you select from your photo library are not stored in the app
  • Photos are not sent over the internet by the app
  • Photos are only used temporarily to create puzzles during your session

Camera Access

The app needs access to your camera to enable you to take photos and use them in the puzzle game.

  • Photos you take using the in-app camera are not stored in the app
  • Photos are not sent over the internet by the app
  • Photos are only used temporarily to create puzzles during your session

Important: All photo processing happens locally on your device. No photos ever leave your device through this app.
